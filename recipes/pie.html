Old Village Bakery Pie

It's hard to beat a fresh pie, especially when it's filled with delicious in-season berries! Follow this recipe to recreate your favorite Old Village Bakery pies. Crust recipe is proprietary and thus not included.

Ingredients

  • Fruit, 2.25 lbs
  • Crust, either double crust or crumb top
  • Sugar, 1 cup
  • Tapioca, 1/4 cup
  • Kosher salt, one pinch

Steps

  1. Roll out crust, and place into pie tin. Crimp edges (if crumb top) and chill in fridge.
  2. Mix pie fruit in bowl with sugar, tapioca, salt, then let sit for at least 10 minutes.
  3. Pour fruit into pie shell, top with crumb (or double crust and crimp).
  4. If double crust, egg wash top, sprinkle with sanding sugar, then score.
  5. Bake for ~60 minutes, starting at 450ºF and lowering to 325ºF after 20 minutes.
  6. Tent with an additional pie tin if it becomes too dark while baking.
  7. Let cool fully before serving.
